### 1. Tier Table

Graduated **bet_max** ladder with 5 tiers, unlocking sequentially. Each tier requires **cumulative trades** (total closed positions in 0.80-0.88 zone) and **rolling 28-day win rate** (WR, wins/closed trades) above threshold. Bankroll floor added from Tier 3 for compounding safety. bet_pct remains 0.06 (position size = min(0.06 * bankroll, bet_max)), but bet_max now scales. Per-market max exposure = bet_max * 2 (DupGuard).

| Tier | Unlock Criteria (cumulative n_trades, min_WR, bankroll_floor) | bet_max ($) |
|------|--------------------------------------------------------------|-------------|
| 1    | n=0, WR=N/A, no floor                                        | 15         |
| 2    | n=50, WR=0.60, no floor                                      | 35         |
| 3    | n=150, WR=0.65, $1,000                                       | 75         |
| 4    | n=400, WR=0.70, $3,000                                       | 150        |
| 5    | n=800, WR=0.75, $7,500                                       | 300        |

- Start at Tier 1 (conservative cap matches current).
- Promote on daily check after criteria met; demote per circuit-breaker below.
- At $10k bankroll on Tier 1: still $15 (safe start). Tier 5: $300 (3% uncapped).

### 2. Drawdown Circuit-Breaker

Auto-drop **one tier** (min Tier 1) on any of these, resetting bet_max immediately. Recheck daily for re-promotion.

- Rolling 14-day WR < 0.50 (catches sharp decline).
- Rolling 28-day profit factor < 1.2 (total profit / total loss; accounts for loss magnitude).
- Bankroll drawdown >15% from 28-day peak (absolute protection).
- Consecutive losses =5 (immediate pause 24h + drop).

Resume trading post-pause only if back to prior tier criteria.

### 3. Kelly/Variance Reasoning

Kelly criterion for bet size: \( f = \frac{p(b+1) - 1}{b} \), where \( p \) = WR, \( b \) = net odds (entry 0.84 avg → exit 0.96 = 14.3% gross edge, fees ~1%; net \( b \approx 0.143 / (1-0.143) \approx 0.167 \)).

| WR (p) | Kelly f (%) | Half-Kelly f (%) | Safe bet_max at $10k bankroll (half-Kelly * 55% alloc * 2 rungs) |
|--------|-------------|------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| 0.65   | 5.9%        | 3.0%             | $330                                                          |
| 0.70   | 8.5%        | 4.3%             | $470                                                          |
| 0.75   | 11.2%       | 5.6%             | $610                                                          |

- Tier 1 ($15 max, 0.16% bankroll at $10k): Safe even at WR=0.55 (half-Kelly ~1.2%).
- Tier 3 ($75): Safe at WR=0.65 (matches unlock).
- Tier 5 ($300): Safe at WR=0.75 (0.55-0.66% bankroll at $10k, < half-Kelly).
- Variance: At WR=0.65, std dev per trade ~25% ROI (14% edge, 20-40% loss cap). 2 rungs = ~0.6-1.2% bankroll risk. 400 trades for Tier 4 gives ~3% WR std error (binomial), confirming stability[1][2].

Higher caps "earned" by data; half-Kelly buffers estimation error and time-decay variance.

### 4. Win Rate Assumption for Tier 1

Use **65% WR prior** for Tier 1 sizing/simulations. Justification:
- Break-even WR = 1 / (1 + net edge) ≈ 63-68% at 14% gross (0.84→0.96, fees/time-decay drag).
- 4-AI consensus + <4h close bonus implies +5-10% edge over SHORT_TERM (44.9% WR at 0.465 entry).
- Bayesian prior: 50/50 base + 15% AI lift = 65%. Matches NEAR_CERTAIN signal strength without overclaiming zero-data zone.
- Conservative: Sim 1000 Monte Carlo runs at 65% WR, 20% avg loss yields +12% bankroll CAGR at Tier 1 sizes.

### 5. Traps/Gotchas

- **Data contamination**: Live WR tainted by geoblocks; reset counters post-migration. Ignore pre-0.80 data for tiers[1].
- **Liquidity thinness**: At $150+ bets, CLOB slippage >2% in low-volume markets. Add pre-check: min $500 depth at entry price[1][2].
- **Time-decay over-reliance**: Ladder assumes 20-40% loss cap, but >4h markets decay slower. Gate harder (e.g., reject if >2h).
- **Tier gaming**: DupGuard limits ladders, but correlated markets could chain exposure. Add total engine exposure cap: 10x bet_max across open positions.
- **Bankroll floor lag**: At hyper-growth, floor binds (e.g., Tier 3 at $900 → stuck). Audit weekly; optional: floor = prev_tier * 1.5.
- **Kelly blowup**: If WR spikes to 80% early, aggressive sizing tempts override. Enforce half-Kelly max f=6% total.
- **Polymarket specifics**: $5 min + 60 orders/min rate limit → ladder fills may partial; track unfilled[1][2].
